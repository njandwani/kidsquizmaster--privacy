Kids Quiz Master — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2025

Kids Quiz Master ("we," "our," or "the app") is an educational quiz game. We take your privacy seriously, especially because our app is used by families and children. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account in the app, we collect the following information:

  • Name — Used to personalize your experience within the app.
  • Email address — Used as your login identifier.
  • Password — Stored only as a secure hash (SHA-256). We never store or transmit your plain-text password.
  • Age — Used to generate age-appropriate quiz questions.

The app also stores the following data as you use it:

  • Quiz results — Your scores, categories played, and dates for progress tracking.
  • Coin balance — In-app currency earned or purchased.

2. Where Your Data Is Stored

All personal data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have a server, cloud database, or any backend system that stores your information. If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • OpenAI API — Used to generate fresh quiz questions. When generating questions, the app sends only the quiz category name (e.g., "Geography"), age, and difficulty level to the OpenAI API. No personal information such as your name, email, or any other identifying data is sent to OpenAI.

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases for virtual coins. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

5. Children's Privacy

Kids Quiz Master is an educational app designed for families. While the app is not exclusively designed for children under 13, it is suitable for use by children with parental supervision.

  • We recommend that children under 13 use the app under the guidance of a parent or guardian.
  • Parents or guardians should create and manage accounts on behalf of young children.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental involvement.
  • All data is stored locally on the device and is never transmitted to our servers (we do not operate any servers).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, you can simply delete the app to remove all data, or delete the profile within the app's settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. The only external communication the app makes is to the OpenAI API for quiz question generation, which does not include any personal data.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are stored using SHA-256 hashing and are never stored in plain text.
  • All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • API communications use HTTPS encryption.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access — View your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion — Delete your profile in the app's settings, or delete the app entirely to remove all data.
  • Portability — Your data is contained within the app on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
